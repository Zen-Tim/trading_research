Executive Summary: The Compositional Shift
The transformation from small to large gaps represents one of the clearest patterns in price action analysis.

The Critical Threshold: The tipping point occurs between 25-50% of ABR. Below this range, breakout patterns dominate and gaps usually close. Above this range, No Overlap patterns dominate and gaps rarely close. For opens above high, the shift is dramatic: a 93 percentage point increase in Bull No Overlap from smallest to largest gaps. For opens below low: a 78 percentage point increase in Bear No Overlap.
Trading Implication: Bar Type acts as a real-time confirmation signal. When you see a large gap (>50% ABR) form a Bull No Overlap or Bear No Overlap bar, the probability the gap stays open through the session exceeds 70%. Conversely, small gaps (<25% ABR) that form Bull Breakout or Bear Breakout bars have an 80%+ chance of closing.
