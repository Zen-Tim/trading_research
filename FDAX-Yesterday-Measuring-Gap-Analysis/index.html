Step 1: What is a Measuring Gap?
A measuring gap occurs when today's entire range does not overlap with yesterday's entire range — pure separation with no touch back. This represents maximum directional conviction.
Measuring Gap Definition
+1 Bull Measuring Gap — Today's low > Yesterday's high
-1 Bear Measuring Gap — Today's high < Yesterday's low
Unlike a simple "gap open" (which may close during the session), a measuring gap maintains complete separation throughout the entire trading day. This is the purest form of gap — no overlap, no touch, pure trend continuation.
